# Overview
If you believe the Christian truth, what does it mean to live a life on the basis of that?

In Romans 12, Paul starts with the word, “Therefore.” It’s a remarkable summary in which Paul says that if we understood the gospel, we’d live a certain way. In a nutshell, Paul gives us the essence of the Christian life.

This passage shows us 1) the essence of the Christian life, 2) some aspects of the Christian life, and 3) the power we need for the Christian life.
