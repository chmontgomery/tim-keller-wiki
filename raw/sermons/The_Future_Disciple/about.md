# Overview
How do you deal with failure? How do you deal with major, epic failure? And come out on the other side a greater person than you were before?

In the Gospel of John, we have the story of Peter’s failure in three parts: Jesus’ prediction, Peter’s betrayal, and Jesus’ restoration of Peter. And in this story, we have a guide for how to deal with failure.

Let’s look at 1) the reasons for Peter’s failure, 2) the ways Jesus healed Peter, and 3) what Peter became.
