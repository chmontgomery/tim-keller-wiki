# Overview
The gospel isn’t the ABCs of the Christian faith; it’s the A to Z. It’s not a set of beliefs or morals but rather a distinct worldview. The gospel is news about what Jesus has already done for us. It’s the way we get through every difficulty, the way we change. This talk was given at a training conference at Redeemer Presbyterian Church in New York City.
