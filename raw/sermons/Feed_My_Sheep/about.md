# Overview
Sometimes, Jesus comes in as a counselor and liberates us from the things that bind us and keep us from being what we should be.

We’ve been looking at how Jesus prepares his disciples to go out into the world, often through teaching. And yet, if you are a follower of Jesus Christ, not only will you need to be taught by him, but you will also need the kind of surgical counseling that he does on Peter.

So let’s take a look at 1) the depth of Peter’s problem, 2) the surgery Jesus does on Peter’s problem, and 3) how it’s healed.
