# Overview
A trait or quality of a supernaturally changed heart is peace.

There’s a difference between a supernaturally changed heart and a morally restrained heart. The marks of a supernaturally changed heart are sometimes called the fruit of the Spirit. They’re listed a number of places in the New Testament. And peace is always included.

So let’s look at 1) what is this supernatural peace that’s available to us? and then 2) how can we cultivate it?
