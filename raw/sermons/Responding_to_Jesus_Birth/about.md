# Overview
The Son of God is born into the world. So what should we do about that?

Luke 2 is maybe the most famous of all the Christmas texts. There’s a lot in it, but let’s ask a simple question: how should we respond to the birth of Jesus Christ?

The three things I think the text tells us we should do are 1) hear well, 2) make peace, and 3) fear not.
