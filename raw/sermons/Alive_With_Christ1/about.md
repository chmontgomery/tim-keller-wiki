# Overview
When we look into the idea of sin, we see why we need to be saved by God’s grace. Understanding sin is key to grasp the good news of salvation and how it relates to our own struggles, even though some churches don’t like to talk about sin. It helps us see the difference between being spiritually alive or dead, recognize our own mistakes, and realize we need a big change in our lives that can only come from being born again and admitting we are sinful.
