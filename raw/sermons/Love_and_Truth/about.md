# Overview
Some people say there’s a cultural crisis of integrity.

For example, Volkswagon was revealed to have deliberately used software designed to lie about emissions. It was a failure of integrity from one of the biggest corporations in the world. And some of you may be yawning, thinking that’s just the way things are. But the Bible says a supernaturally changed heart rejoices with the truth.

Let’s talk about 1) how important integrity is, 2) how you practice integrity, and 3) how you can become people of integrity.
