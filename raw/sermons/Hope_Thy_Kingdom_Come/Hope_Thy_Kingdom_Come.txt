Thanks father. For giving us. What we need. In order to. Pray. Thy kingdom. Come. And we ask. That as we pray it. We would feel. Your kingdom power. He would sense. Your kingdom power. Extending itself. Through us. And healing us. Of all that's wrong. With us. Teach us. As a community. Teach us. As individuals. To say. Thy kingdom. Come. In us. In the world. In me. We pray this. In Jesus name. Amen.
