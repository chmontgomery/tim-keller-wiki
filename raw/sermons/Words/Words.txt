All those Proverbs that say the tongue of the wise is healing. And we ask that you would help us to be agents of that healing in this world. Fill our hearts with the Pentecostal power. Fill our hearts with Jesus, your son, and what he's done for us, and the love that is ours through him, so that we can, even if we're stones, cry out in praise and be instruments of healing in the world. We pray this in Jesus' name. Amen.
