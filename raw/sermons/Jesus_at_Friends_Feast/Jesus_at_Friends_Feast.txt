We ask that you would help us to, though, begin to experience some of that so we can have our lives absolutely shaped from the inside out by experience of your love and your grace and your goodness. And we thank you for ways in which you've shown us today in this passage that we can approach that. And we ask that you would, by your Holy Spirit, empower us to do what we now purpose in our hearts to do. We ask all this through Jesus. In his name we pray. Amen.
