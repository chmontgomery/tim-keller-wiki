# Overview
This is one of the most famous passages in the Bible. It’s about what’s really in the human heart, and I’ve pondered this passage over the years. The main thing I should say is woe be unto us if we don’t learn what this passage is seeking to teach us. It teaches us at least three things:the power of sin,the shrewdness of grace, andthe assurance of pardon.
