# Overview
I thought I believed in the resurrection. Then I got diagnosed with thyroid cancer. And it turned out I did, but I didn’t. Or maybe I should say, my faith in the resurrection of Jesus Christ was not as consoling, empowering, and life-transforming as it should have been.

Thomas is the most famous doubter of the resurrection. So when we see him coming from skepticism into faith, that helps us all.

Let’s look at the famous account of Thomas and ask the questions: 1) why didn’t Thomas believe? and 2) how did he come to believe?
