# Overview
We’ve been looking at the animating gospel principles that have shaped Redeemer Presbyterian’s life in the city.

Essentially, we’ve been looking at some aspects of what the church does. But in this unparalleled passage in 1 Peter, we’re going to learn not so much what the church does, but what the church is.

1 Peter gives us insight into the church’s 1) glory, 2) gifts, and 3) grace.
