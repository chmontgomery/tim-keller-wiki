We are facing other things, and as a result, we are bringing death into the world. we pray that you would heal us with the image of your son dying on the cross which shows us your glory in such a way that we finally have our hearts healed and as the image of God is healed in us we begin to honor it in others make us a church filled with people who speak graciously to each other make us a church that is so committed to the importance of human life make us a church that cares about the weak and cares about the marginal and cares about the powerless about the women and the children and cares about people who the world has cast off. Make us a church in which we love each other and speak graciously to each other regardless of where we're from. Make us a church that understands the image of God because your son was conformed to our likeness on the cross of sinful flesh that we might be conformed into his likeness forever and be made glorious as you are glorious. We pray all this in Jesus' name. Amen. Thank you.
