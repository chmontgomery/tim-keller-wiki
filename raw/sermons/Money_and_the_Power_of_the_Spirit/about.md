# Overview
We’re looking at how Christianity affects your attitude and practice with regard to your money.

This passage from Acts is notable because it depicts powerful spiritual experience and then it immediately shows the impact of Christian spiritual experience on the most practical thing: the way you deploy, spend, and regard your money and your possessions.

Let’s look at 1) what this text teaches about spiritual experience, 2) what impact the text has on the way we regard our money and possessions.
