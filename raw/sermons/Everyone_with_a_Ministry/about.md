# Overview
The church described in 1 Peter 2 is the church we seek to become.

Though I know there are plenty of you who aren’t really sure where you stand when it comes to faith, I still think you will find this helpful.

This text 1) assumes a dynamic of gospel ministry, 2) tells us who the bearers of that gospel ministry are, and 3) shows us how to get the power for that gospel ministry.
