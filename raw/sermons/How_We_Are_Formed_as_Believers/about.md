# Overview
Psalm 1 introduces the book of Psalms with a prayer. What is the secret of life-forming prayer? How does meditation work? What can we learn about meditation from Jesus, and even from others such as Martin Luther? The sermon shows us the way into prayer, the way through prayer, and the way all true prayer results in praise.
