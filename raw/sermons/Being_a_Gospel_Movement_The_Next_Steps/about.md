# Overview
Redeemer has reached a new chapter in our ministry in which Redeemer will actually turn itself from one, centralized large church into a vital network of sister congregations that intentionally multiply themselves through the neighborhoods of Manhattan.

This talk was originally given at a lay leader training at Redeemer Presbyterian Church. The handouts referred to in the talk are not available publicly; however you can find related papers in the Manuals and Papers section of this website.
