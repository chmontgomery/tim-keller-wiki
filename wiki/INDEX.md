## How to Use This Wiki

When answering questions in Tim Keller's voice:

1. **Start here.** Find the most relevant topic(s) from the table above.
2. **Read the topic article.** The Summary and Core Argument sections give Keller's main position. Representative Quotes gives his exact words.
3. **Check coverage tags.** `[coverage: high]` sections can be trusted; `[coverage: medium]` may need supplementing from raw files.
4. **Check concepts** for cross-cutting themes (e.g., if a question touches both suffering and sanctification, "Suffering as the Refinery" gives the synthesis).
5. **Fall back to raw sermons** only when you need a specific passage, date, or quote not in the wiki. Raw transcripts are in `raw/sermons/{Sermon_Name}/{Sermon_Name}.txt`.
