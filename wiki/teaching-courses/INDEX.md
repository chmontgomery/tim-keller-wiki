# Teaching Courses — Tim Keller on Preaching

Compiled meta-content from Keller's lectures on **how to preach** — not Keller's preaching itself. Use this section when the question is about **sermon craft, preaching method, or pastoral self-formation**, not when the question is about a theological topic Keller addresses in his sermons (for that, use `wiki/topics/`).

## Courses compiled

### Preaching Christ in a Postmodern World (TGC, originally a Doctor of Ministry course)

Co-taught by Tim Keller and Edmund Clowney. Only the 9 Keller-led sessions are transcribed and compiled here; the other 9 Clowney sessions are out of scope for this wiki. The course's central frame: every sermon should make Jesus *visible*, not merely *talk about* him, via the triad **Expounding Christ → Applying Christ → Adoring Christ**.

| Topic | Method area | Sources |
|---|---|---|
| [Christocentric Preaching — Course Overview](topics/christocentric-preaching-overview.md) | Foundational frame | Session 1 |
| [Christ-Centered Application](topics/christ-centered-application.md) | Getting from any text to Christ (themes, types, four ways) | Sessions 4–5 |
| [Application Strategy](topics/application-strategy.md) | Macro-strategies, audience differentiation, the three pitfalls | Sessions 8–9 |
| [Preaching to Worldviews](topics/preaching-to-worldviews.md) | Subversive fulfillment, woven-in apologetics, cultural engagement | Sessions 12–13 |
| [The Preacher's Heart](topics/the-preachers-heart.md) | Spiritual reality, gifts vs. grace, communion with God | Sessions 16, 18 |

## How to use these articles

Each topic article has:
- **Summary** — the core thesis in 2–3 paragraphs
- **The Practice / specific method sections** — the actionable how-to
- **Pitfalls to Avoid** — Keller's warnings
- **Worked Examples** — specific passages or cases Keller works through
- **Connection to Keller's Own Preaching** — cross-links into `wiki/topics/` for where you can see the method in practice in his actual sermons
- **Sources** — back to the raw lecture transcripts under `raw/teaching-courses/`

For full transcripts and lecture metadata, see `raw/teaching-courses/christocentric-preaching/`.
